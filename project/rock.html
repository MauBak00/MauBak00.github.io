Overview

Rock music is a very popular and energetic genre of music that evolved musically from the blues. Its earliest form was rock and roll, but rock music has since evolved into a wide variety of styles, ranging widely in complexity and feel. Rock music generally uses a 4/4 time signature and a verse-chorus form, and it is usually performed by electric guitarists and bassists, drummers, and singers.
